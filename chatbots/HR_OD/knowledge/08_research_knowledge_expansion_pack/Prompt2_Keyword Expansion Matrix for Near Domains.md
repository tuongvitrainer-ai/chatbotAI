## Gợi ý dùng cho Facet Matrix

Từ bộ keyword Near, nên tạo Facet Matrix theo 6 facet:

1. **Interface facet:** Policy / L&D / Analytics / Legal / DEI / Communication / PM / Strategy.
2. **Trigger facet:** Khi nào cần kéo Near domain vào OD answer.
3. **Boundary facet:** Khi nào phải dừng ở note/checklist/escalation.
4. **Artifact facet:** Executive memo / Manager guide / Employee FAQ / Rollout plan / HR-Legal checklist.
5. **Data facet:** Survey / HR metrics / Listening / Feedback / Aggregated data / Anonymized comments.
6. **Risk facet:** Legal risk / Individual judgment / Sensitive data / Scope drift / One-way communication / Overclaim.
